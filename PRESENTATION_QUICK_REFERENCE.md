## 🚫 Common Questions & Answers

**Q: What if the AI is wrong?**  
A: Every score is reviewed by a human judge who can override.

**Q: Is it biased?**  
A: AI uses standardized rubric. Human oversight ensures fairness.

**Q: How accurate is it?**  
A: 90%+ judge acceptance rate with <5 point average difference.

**Q: What about edge cases?**  
A: Judges handle nuanced situations. AI handles routine evaluation.
